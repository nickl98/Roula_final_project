What is Roula?

Roula is a start-up company that is behind the movement of data compensation. You may be wondering what "Data-Compensation" is however, its main goal is to take the money that we deserve from the Tech giants. Roula's main goal upon opening is that we will be paying our users with the money we earn from our user's engagement. For years we keep seeing how big and powerful these companies are and users are left with nothing but obtrusive advertisements.
